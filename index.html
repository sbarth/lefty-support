Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete a transaction?

To delete a transaction, open the transaction screen and swipe left on the entry you want to remove. A menu will appear allowing you to delete the expense.

Alternatively, you can swipe completely to the left and the transaction will be removed immediately.

Is Lefty available on Android?

No. Lefty is currently available exclusively for iOS.

Does Lefty store my personal data?

Lefty is designed with privacy in mind. If your app processes personal data, details will be provided in the privacy policy.
